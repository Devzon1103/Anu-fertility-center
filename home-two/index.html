A gynecological ultrasound is a safe, non-invasive imaging procedure used to assess the health of the female reproductive organs, including the uterus, ovaries, and fallopian tubes. It helps diagnose conditions such as ovarian cysts, uterine fibroids, pelvic pain, and irregular bleeding.
