FAQs

Your questions, answered

What services does the clinic provide?

We offer a range of services including general check-ups, diagnostics, vaccinations, specialized treatments, and wellness programs.

Do I need an appointment, or do you accept walk-ins?

We accept both scheduled appointments and walk-ins. However, we recommend booking an appointment to reduce your wait time.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance providers. Please contact us in advance to confirm if your plan is covered.

How do I prepare for my visit?

Bring a valid ID, insurance information, and any medical records if available. For specific tests, we will provide preparation guidelines in advance.
