What services does the clinic provide?

Our clinic offers a range of services, including preventive care (Pap smears, mammograms, and general health screenings), reproductive health (fertility counseling, contraception, and menopause management), prenatal and postpartum care, hormonal health evaluations, and mental health support. Please contact us for a full list of services.

Do I need an appointment, or do you accept walk-ins?

Appointments are preferred to ensure you receive personalized and timely care. However, we do accept walk-ins for urgent issues or minor consultations based on availability. It’s best to call ahead to confirm wait times.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance providers, including private health plans, Medicare, and Medicaid. If you’re unsure whether we accept your plan, our administrative team is happy to assist. Payment plans are also available for those without insurance.

Can I request a specific provider for my visit?

Absolutely! If you have a preferred doctor or nurse practitioner, let us know when scheduling your appointment. We will do our best to accommodate your request, depending on availability.

How do I prepare for my visit?

For most appointments, bring your ID, insurance card, and a list of any medications you’re currently taking. For specific procedures like Pap smears or lab tests, our team will provide detailed preparation instructions when you book your visit.
